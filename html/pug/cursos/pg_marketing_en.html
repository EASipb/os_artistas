Marketing Digital

Profile of the study program

The digital transformation of the last decades has revolutionized the way people relate to information. Today's reality focuses on information as the competitive and differentiating engine of organizations. The volume, complexity, structuring and mechanisms for providing information today require a much more demanding profile from the marketing professional, with regard to the tools he has to work in a more analytical way and with a very demanding time-to-market.

The Post-Graduation in Digital Marketing aims to create assets with a 360° vision focused on the customer and the definition of differentiating local and global strategies, with the potential to generate competitive advantages for organizations, integrating knowledge and technological skills to streamline and optimize data-driven decision-making.

Occupational profile of graduates

The Post-Graduation in Digital Marketing aims to provide students with training that allows them, through a strategically defined curricular structure, to acquire skills that allow them to design, create, plan and execute marketing campaigns and activities based on the digital domains and based on the use of data and all types of organizations.
