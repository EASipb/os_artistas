Legal Services

The diploma of higher professional technician in Legal Services is awarded to students who demonstrate, in this area:

a) possess knowledge and comprehension skills;

b) know how to apply, in professional contexts, the knowledge and comprehension skills acquired;

c) have the ability to identify and use information to respond to well-defined concrete and abstract problems;

d) possess skills that enable them to communicate about their understanding of issues, skills and activities with their peers, supervisors and clients;

e) Possess learning skills that allow them to pursue studies with some autonomy.

Profile of the study program

Plan, coordinate and execute activities of a legal nature in public or private organizations, contributing to the administrative management of these entities and to a faster and more efficient judicial system.

Occupational profile of graduates

The Higher Technical Professional Course in Legal Services is part of the area of Law training, and its graduates are prepared to perform technical functions, within the scope of the study program profile:

- Plan, organize, execute and control the administrative activities inherent to the functional areas of the professionals or public and private organizations in which they provide services.

- Manage processes and schedule procedural acts.

- Manage, autonomously, the workflow inherent to the operation of the administrative and secretarial services.

- Manage the activities related to the customer service.

- Manage and promote the payment and allocation of procedural costs.

- Manage the information received and produced, applying the computer tools to support the professional activity.

- Manage, autonomously, the workflow inherent to the operation of the administrative and secretarial services.

- Coordinate the tasks related to the external office service.

- Create and/or organize a document archive.

- Coordinate procedural management in the human resources, accounting and litigation departments of organizations.

- Design, develop and monitor the implementation of mechanisms to support citizens for the realization of their legal rights and duties.
