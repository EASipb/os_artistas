Competencies

01

To assess the plan of scientific activities of the School;

02

To pronounce on the creation, transformation or extinction of the Institute's organic units;

03

To deliberate on the distribution of the teaching service;

04

To pronounce on the creation of courses and approve the study plans of the courses taught;

05

Proper or pronounce on the granting of honorary titles or distinctions, the institution of school prizes and the establishment of international agreements and partnerships;

06

Propose the composition of the juries of examinations and academic competitions;

07

Approve the accreditation of certified training and professional experience, for the purpose of continuing studies, under the terms of the law and regulations in force;

08

Propose the number of vacancies per course;

09

To pronounce on the recognition of degrees and diplomas, requests for equivalence to scholarship holders, scholarships, exemptions from teaching service and integration in research units and research teams.
