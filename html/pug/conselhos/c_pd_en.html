Pedagogical Council

The Pedagogical Council is organized in Course Committees and in Assemblies, instances functionally articulated within the scope of the IPB Statutes. The Assembly of the Pedagogical Council is made up of the President of the Pedagogical Council, the Course Directors and one student from each course.

Diretora - Sónia Paula da Silva Nogueira
Luisa Margarida Barata Lopes

Competencies

01

To pronounce on the pedagogical guidelines and the teaching and assessment methods;

02

Promote regular surveys of the pedagogical performance of the School or Institute, carry out their analysis and dissemination and propose measures for improvement;

03

Promote the evaluation of the pedagogical performance of teachers, by them and by students, carry out its analysis and dissemination and propose improvement measures;

04

To assess complaints regarding pedagogical failures, and to propose the necessary measures;

05

Approve the regulation for the evaluation of students' performance;

06

To pronounce on the statute of limitations;

07

To pronounce on the creation of courses and on the plans of the courses taught;

08

To pronounce on the institution of school awards;

09

To pronounce on the academic calendar and the School's examination maps;

10

Liaise with the Student Ombudsman.
