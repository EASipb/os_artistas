Why EsACT?

  • EsACT-IPB is an integral part of the Polytechnic Institute of Bragança (IPB), known for its academic quality and excellence in research.
  • Our courses are taught by experienced and dedicated faculty, providing a solid and relevant education.
  • Located in Mirandela, EsACT-IPB is situated in a region rich in culture, history, and tourist opportunities.
  • Students can explore the surrounding environment and apply their knowledge in real-world contexts.
  • EsACT-IPB provides a unique opportunity for students to connect with peers, faculty, and industry professionals. This network of contacts can be valuable for future job opportunities and collaborations.
  • EsACT-IPB's courses cover various areas, allowing students to develop diverse skills and become versatile professionals.

FAQ

How to apply for higher education?

The application for higher education is made only on the Online Application platform of the Directorate-General for Higher Education (DGES). The first phase takes place between July and August of each year (there are a total of three phases of competition). To apply, you need an access password and the ENES Form of the year in which you are applying.

How to request documents related to the application for higher education?

To obtain documents related to the application, access the forms area on the website of the Directorate-General for Higher Education. Select the type of document required, fill out the form and make the payment using the ATM reference indicated. The document will be sent to the email address you indicated.

Who can apply for higher education?

In the general regime, students who meet the following requirements can apply for higher education in 2023:

Have passed a secondary course under the conditions for further studies, if any, or legally equivalent qualification

When can you apply for higher education?

The national competition for access has three application phases, defined in an annual calendar. You can apply in all three phases. Consult the annual calendar of the national competition for access to higher education to find out the application deadlines.

What are the documents and requirements to apply for higher education?

To apply, you will need:

From the ENES (National Higher Education Exams) form.

Authenticate yourself on the application platform using one of the following ways: access password, Digital Mobile Key or Citizen Card with PIN codes and a card reader. The ENES form contains the student's final grade in secondary education and the grades obtained in the entrance exams.

Where can I find more information about higher education applications?

Please refer to the website of the Directorate-General for Higher Education for specific details on competitions, vacancies, requirements and procedures.
